Hovermarks

Quickly and easily annotate a bookshelf




Hovermarks are a tool for annotating a bookshelf. They help you highlight a collection without relying on stickers or other more permanent tools.

They're cheap. They're easy. They're ephemeral. They're fun.

Hovermarks are papercraft. You print out a pattern, cut, and fold.

How they work

You select a patterrn, fold it, and then slip them between the pages. They're nothing more than a bookmark that faces the spine of the book.

Libraries can use them to hightlight collections in the stacks. Beach reads anyone? How about showing off some local authors?
