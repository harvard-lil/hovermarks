Quickly and easily annotate a bookshelf

Hovermarks?

Hovermarks are tool for annotating bookshelves.

They're cheap. They're easy. They're ephemeral. They're fun.

Hovermarks are papercraft. You print out a pattern, cut, and fold.

How they work

Hovermarks are papercraft. They're nothing more than a bookmark that faces the spine of the book.
