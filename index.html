Easy and free

Hovermarks provide new ways to interact with the collection. They allow for temporary, lightweight annotation without moving the items off the shelf.

Print, fold, cut. Libraries. Bookshops. You.

Hovermarks are produced by libraries and are openly licensed and their sources are freely available.
