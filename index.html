Hovermarks

Quickly and easily annotate a bookshelf

Hovermarks are a tool for annotating a bookshelf. They help you highlight a collection without relying on stickers or other more permanent tools.

They're cheap. They're easy. They're ephemeral. They're fun.

Hovermarks are papercraft. No sticker goo here.

Hovermarks in your library

Hovermarks are nothing more than a bookmark turned 90° They mark a place in the stacks, bringing life to a stale section.

Use them to highlight a collection. Beach reads anyone? How about showing off some local authors?

Plan an event -- a group of kids get to craft two Hovermarks and then they're turned loose in the children's room to mark a book they read recently and a book they want to checkout in the future.
